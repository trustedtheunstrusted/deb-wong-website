Root: The Soul Document

根基方案：灵魂档案

Planting the Essence, Defining the DNA.

种下本质，定义基因。

What this is这是什么

A brand is not "designed"; it is "remembered." The Soul Document is an archeological journey into the founder's vision — finding the root before anything grows above ground.

品牌不是被"设计"出来的，而是被"记住"的。灵魂档案是一场深入创始人愿景的考古之旅——在任何东西长出地面之前，先找到那条根。

Best for适合谁

Founders who know what they do but struggle to say who they are. You have a real product or service — you just need the language and framework to bring it into the world.

知道自己在做什么，却说不清自己是谁的创始人。你有真材实料——你只是需要把它带入世界的语言和框架。

What you'll receive交付内容

  • Deep-Dive Discovery Session (2 hrs)

    深度发现访谈 （2小时）

    A recorded interview to excavate your brand's true origin, values, and vision.

    录音深度访谈，挖掘品牌真实起源、价值观与愿景。

  • Brand Core Document

    品牌核心文件

    Mission, vision, values, and brand personality — distilled into one clear reference you'll use for years.

    使命、愿景、价值观、品牌人格——凝练成一份你会用很多年的核心文件。

  • Positioning Statement

    品牌定位语句

    One sentence that captures who you are, who you serve, and why it matters.

    一句话定义你是谁、服务谁、为何重要。

  • Language & Voice Guide

    语言风格指南

    Key messages, tone of voice, and words to use (and avoid) across all communications.

    核心表达、语气风格，以及传播中该用和该避开的词汇。

"This document becomes your foundation for everything that follows — every brief, every collaboration, every piece of content."

「这份文件将成为你此后一切的基石——每一份简报、每一次合作、每一篇内容。」
